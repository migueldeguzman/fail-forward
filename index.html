Fail Forward Project

Shape your failures and turn them into success building blocks

I am not a psychologist (I'm actually a Retired Accountant) but I encountered depression and anxiety after failing my first business endeavor. I got immersed in the trap of social media: posting and sharing the beauty of the island life, filming and uploading drone videos, the best sunset photos and meeting great and beautiful people, all of these are seemingly great indicators of a joyful life but unfortunately it was not and it's like a whack in the head of a baseball bat. I'm drinking alcohol almost everyday for a year and got my immune system to its lowest, resulting to a ton of skin diseases all over my body. It destroyed my confidence and altered my behavior. The chase for profits and recoginition became a reason for my "ultimate failure". I know how it feels to get lost and get stuck. The experience of crashing and burning became essential to the concepts you are reading now.

I want to help you get unstuck and move forward. Your failure have affected you and caused your mind to create a memory loop of depression and forward intuition of what you might fail in the future. A lot of great people wasted their lives even at the peak of their successes doing the same thing. Anthony Bourdain, Chester Bennington, these are just two of the recent famous people who decided end their lives after going through depression. A lot of people are encountering this very problem now. Various reports show that suicide rates are at all time high all over the world.

Your story is for sure different, but I am fortunate enough to have a chance to help you get "unstuck" through the methods I adapted to bring myself back to reality. Before I get to the details of, let's dive first on understanding why all of us fail in the first place.
